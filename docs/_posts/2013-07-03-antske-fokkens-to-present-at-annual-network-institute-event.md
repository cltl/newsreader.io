[Antske Fokkens](http://www.coli.uni-saarland.de/~afokkens/) will present at the [Annual Network Institute Event](http://www.networkinstitute.org/news/annual-network-institute-event-july-4-2013/) on July 4. During this event, the different groups working together in the interdisciplinary [Network Institute](http://www.networkinstitute.org/) at [VU University](http://www.vu.nl) will come together to exchange their research ideas and results. Antske will describe her attempts at reproducing some research published by others in her field, using the original data. And guess how easy that was…
