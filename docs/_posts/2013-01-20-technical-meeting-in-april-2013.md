General Information about [Donostia – San Sebastián](http://www.donostia.org/taxo.nsf/fwHomeCanal?ReadForm&idioma=ing&id=B&doc=Canal).

All Spanish airports are managed by [AENA](http://www.aena.es/csee/Satellite?pagename=Home&Language=EN_GB):

There you will find information about transport facilities from your arrival airport to San Sebastián.  
+ San Sebastián: there are taxis and public buses.  
+ Bilbao: there is a direct bus from the airport to San Sebastián. It takes one hour and 15€. Then, a taxi is the best option to your hotel.
