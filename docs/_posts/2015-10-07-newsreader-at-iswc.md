With Semantic Web technology being a huge part of NewsReader, it is no wonder that we will showcase some of our technology at the [14th International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC)](http://iswc2015.semanticweb.org/) next week in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania.

Here’s a roundup of the sessions in which NewsReader is involved.

**Sunday 11 October**

*[The third NLP&DBpedia workshop](https://nlpdbpedia2015.wordpress.com/)*(location: **RBC 91**)*:* This workshop combines the two main themes in NewsReader, namely Natural Language Processing and Semantic Web.NewsReader team member Marieke van Erp is a co-organiser of this workshop and she will present the position paper “[Missing Mr. Brown and buying an Abraham Lincoln – Dark Entities and DBpedia](https://nlpdbpedia2015.files.wordpress.com/2015/08/nlpdbpedia_2015_submission_14.pdf)” (Marieke van Erp, Filip Ilievski, Marco Rospocher and Piek Vossen)

**Monday 12 October**

Filip Ilievski will present the paper “[LOTUS: Linked Open Text UnleaShed](http://ceur-ws.org/Vol-1426/paper-06.pdf)” (Filip Ilievski, Wouter Beek, Marieke van Erp, Laurens Rietveld and Stefan Schlobach) at the [Sixth Consuming Linked Data Workshop](http://www.dcc.uchile.cl/cold2015/). **12:00 – 12:20** in room **RBC** **91**.

In the afternoon, Marieke van Erp will be co-organising the [Linked Science](http://linkedscience.org/events/lisc2015/) workshop, which is on the use of linked data for publishing, sharing and interlinking scientific resources, data and complete experiments. **14:00 – 17:30** in room **RBC 241**.

**Tuesday 13 October**

Marco Rospocher will present the following two demos at the Poster and Demos session (**18:30 – 21:00**, location: **Vision Bar and Bucks**)

- [Demonstrating the Power of Streaming and Sorting for Non-distributed RDF Processing: RDFpro](https://dkm-static.fbk.eu/people/rospocher/files/pubs/2015iswcdemo2.pdf) (Francesco Corcoglioniti, Alessio Palmero Aprosio, Marco Rospocher) – [rdfpro.fbk.eu](http://rdfpro.fbk.eu/)
- [Extracting Knowledge from Text with PIKES](https://dkm-static.fbk.eu/people/rospocher/files/pubs/2015iswcdemo1.pdf) (Francesco Corcoglioniti, Marco Rospocher, Alessio Palmero Aprosio) – [pikes.fbk.eu](http://pikes.fbk.eu/)
