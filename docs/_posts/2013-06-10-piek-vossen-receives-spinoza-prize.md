Today, the chairman of the Netherlands Organisation for Scientific Research (NWO) announced that Piek Vossen is one of the three scientists this year to receive the highest award in Dutch science: the NWO Spinoza prize. Skip to the NWO website for the  [official press release](http://www.nwo.nl/en/news-and-events/news/2013/nwo-spinoza-prizes-2013-for-mikhail-katsnelson-piek-vossen-and-bert-weckhuysen.html).
