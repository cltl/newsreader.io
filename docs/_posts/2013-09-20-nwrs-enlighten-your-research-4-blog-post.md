The NewsReader consortium is currently preparing a full proposal for [Enlighten Your Research 4](http://www.surfsites.nl/eyr/) (EYR4). Participating in EYR4 would provide us with more computing power, and also very important, advice from the experts running these big machines on how to best use this computing power. During the competition every finalist (i.e. team who made it through the first round) is invited to write a blog post for SurfNet about the project. This morning [NewsReader's post](https://blog.surfnet.nl/?p=2572) was published.
