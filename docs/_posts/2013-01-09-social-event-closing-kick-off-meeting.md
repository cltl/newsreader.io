We organized a special social event as a kick-off for the project:

On January 15, 2013, we will meet at the [Grand Cafe Amstelhoeck](http://www.amstelhoeck.nl/) (formerly known as Cafe Dantzig), next to the main entrance of Stopera/Muziektheater Amsterdam (Amstel 1, 1011PN Amsterdam.

[![la-reine-rondvaart-grachten (1)](/assets/uploads/2013/01/la-reine-rondvaart-grachten-1.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2013/01/la-reine-rondvaart-grachten-1.jpg)

Around 8.00 p.m. we will embark on the classic boat “La Reine” (<http://www.classicboattours.eu/lareine/>) and we will sail through the most beautiful canals of Amsterdam and enjoy a dinner and have some drinks. During the 1st Amsterdam Light Festival (<http://www.amsterdamlightfestival.com/en/> from December 7 till January 20) it’s even more special: lots of enlighted boats and houses along the Amstel river.

[![lf](/assets/uploads/2013/01/lf.jpg)](/assets/uploads/2013/01/lf.jpg)

Around 10.00 p.m. we will arrive at the pier of the Stopera.
